Privacy Policy

CCNAQuest — Last updated: 20 May 2026

CCNAQuest (“the App”, “we”, “us”) is a CCNA exam preparation app developed by James Dagless trading as Cawarden. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

CCNAQuest is designed to work entirely offline. All learning progress is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate user accounts, and we do not transmit your personal information to our servers.

The App stores the following data on your device only using your device’s local storage:

  • Your XP (experience points) and level
  • Your daily streak count and last activity date
  • Your heart (life) count and refill timer
  • Which lessons and sections you have completed
  • Your spaced-repetition review schedule (SM-2 algorithm data)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly back it up via your device’s system backup (e.g. iCloud Backup or Google One Backup), which is governed by Apple’s or Google’s own privacy policies.

2. Crash Reporting

The App uses Sentry (sentry.io) for crash and error reporting. When the App crashes or encounters an unexpected error, Sentry automatically collects limited technical information to help us fix the issue. This may include:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • A stack trace describing the error
  • Approximate geographic region (country level, derived from IP address)

Sentry does not collect your name, email address, learning progress, or any other personal information. Crash reports are used solely to diagnose and fix technical issues. Sentry’s own privacy policy is available at sentry.io/privacy.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Your name, email address, or any account credentials
  • Your location (beyond the country-level crash report above)
  • Your contacts, photos, camera, or microphone
  • Any payment information (the App does not process payments directly)
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking data

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Sentry — error and crash reporting (see Section 2)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — app distribution and in-app purchases if applicable, governed by their respective policies

We do not use advertising networks, analytics platforms, or data brokers.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the address below and we will take steps to delete it.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Since all progress data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by uninstalling the App. Crash report data held by Sentry is automatically deleted after 90 days in accordance with Sentry’s data retention policy.

7. Your Rights

Because we do not hold personal data on our servers, there is typically no data for us to provide, correct, or delete on your behalf. If you have questions about crash report data held by Sentry, you may contact us and we will coordinate with Sentry on your behalf.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights under the GDPR including the right to access, rectify, and erase your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
